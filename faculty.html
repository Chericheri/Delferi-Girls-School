Our Teaching Approach

At Delfari Girls, our faculty employs evidence-based pedagogical methods tailored for technical education:

  • Project-Based Learning

    Students learn by building real applications, not just through lectures

  • Peer Programming

    Collaborative coding develops teamwork skills used in industry

  • Flipped Classroom

    Lecture materials are reviewed at home, class time is for hands-on practice

"We don't just teach code - we teach problem-solving and confidence"
