Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

What are the admission requirements?

Requirements vary by program. For certificate programs, you need a high school diploma. Diploma programs require a minimum C+ in KCSE with good math scores. No prior tech experience is needed for beginner courses - just enthusiasm to learn!

Do you offer financial aid or scholarships?

Yes! We offer several scholarship opportunities including:

  • Women in Tech Scholarship (up to 50% tuition)
  • STEM Achievers Award (for top high school graduates)
  • Need-based financial aid
Contact our admissions office for eligibility details.
